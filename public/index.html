How it works

Set it up once. Remi handles the rest.

No app for your customers to download. No scripts to record. Just a phone number, WhatsApp, and your calendar.

1

Customer calls or messages

Someone calls your business or sends a WhatsApp - even at 10pm on a Sunday.

2

Remi responds instantly

Answers the call with a natural South African voice, or replies on WhatsApp within seconds. Checks availability, answers questions, and books directly into your calendar.

3

It keeps you booked & paid

Reminders cut no-shows. In the background Remi fills quiet slots, wins back lapsed regulars and asks happy clients for reviews - proposed for your approval - then chases unpaid invoices automatically until you're paid.

4

You see the numbers

A live dashboard shows every booking Remi made and the rand value recovered - so you always know your ROI.
