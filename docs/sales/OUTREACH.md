# Remi — Outreach Pack

Ready-to-send scripts to book your first demos. Keep it personal — use the
clinic's name, mention something specific (their Instagram, a service). Lead with
the *problem* (missed bookings), not the tech.

> Goal of every first message: **get a yes to a free 2-week trial / quick demo**,
> not to explain AI.

---

## 5. Discovery call script (10 minutes)

**Opener (the hook):**
> "Quick question — roughly how many calls do you reckon the clinic misses in a
> week, especially when slammed or after hours? Most places miss ~4 in 10, and
> every missed new-patient call is ~R300+ today and a lot more over a year. I've
> built something that answers every call 24/7, books people straight into your
> calendar, and texts back anyone you miss. Let me set it up free for two weeks and
> show you how many bookings it catches."

**Discovery questions (let them talk — the pain sells it):**
1. How many calls do you miss in a week? After hours?
2. What's your no-show rate — and how do you fill an empty slot today?
3. What's a new patient worth on the first visit? Over a year?
4. Who handles bookings now, and what system do you use? (Fresha / GoodX / diary?)

**The close:**
> "Here's what I'll do — I set it up, no cost, two weeks alongside your front desk.
> Then I show you the bookings it caught. If you like the number, it's R[tier]/mo;
> if not, we turn it off. Fair?"

**Then:** get the booking number + booking system, and send their trial link.

## 6. Objection handling

- **"We don't miss that many calls."** → "That's exactly what the free trial shows —
  if it catches nothing, you've lost nothing. Most owners are surprised."
- **"Is it a robot? Will it sound off-brand?"** → "It's natural and on-brand — you can
  hear it live on our site ('Talk to Remi'). We tune the voice to your clinic."
- **"We already use Fresha/[system]."** → "Perfect — Remi works alongside it; it just
  answers the calls and enquiries Fresha can't."
- **"Is patient data safe?"** → "Yes — POPIA-conscious, EU-hosted, and you sign a
  standard operator agreement. Happy to send it."
- **"Too busy to set up."** → "That's the point — it's done-for-you. I need 10 minutes
  of info and I handle the rest."
