### What we collect
- Your **name** and **phone / WhatsApp number**
- The **content of your messages and calls** with Remi
- **Appointment details** (service, date, time, status)
- Anything else you choose to tell us during the conversation

We do **not** ask for clinical or medical details. Please don't share sensitive
health information in chat — discuss that with the Clinic directly.

### Why we process it (and our lawful basis)
- To **answer your enquiry and book, change or cancel appointments** — necessary to
  provide the service you requested.
- To **send you appointment reminders and waitlist offers** — to perform the
  booking service and with your consent given when you first message us.
- To **report aggregate activity** to the Clinic (e.g. number of bookings).

### Who we share it with
The Clinic (your appointment is theirs), and the service providers that make Remi
work: our telephony/WhatsApp provider (Twilio), our voice provider (ElevenLabs),
our database (Supabase), our AI provider (Google / Anthropic), our hosting
(Railway), and calendar (Google, if used). Some of these process data **outside
South Africa**; we only use providers bound to protections substantially similar
to POPIA, or where another lawful ground under POPIA s72 applies.

### How long we keep it
We keep your information only as long as needed to provide the service and meet
legal obligations, then delete or anonymise it. Retention period: 24 months after
your last interaction (unless a longer period is required by law).

### Your rights (POPIA)
You may request **access** to, or **correction or deletion** of, your personal
information, and you may **object** to processing. To exercise these, contact our
Information Officer above. You may also complain to the **Information Regulator
(South Africa)** — enquiries@inforegulator.org.za.
