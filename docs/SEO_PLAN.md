# Remi — SEO & Content Plan

> Reality check: for your first ~10 clinics, **direct outreach + ads win** — SEO is
> a 6–12 month compounding play. Run it in parallel; don't wait on it to sell.

## ✅ Done (on-page technical SEO)
- `<title>` + **meta description** (with "South Africa" + key terms)
- **Canonical** URL, `robots` meta, Open Graph + Twitter card, favicon
- **Schema.org JSON-LD** — Organization + WebSite + Service (with the 3 pricing offers)
- `public/robots.txt` (blocks `/dashboard`, `/report`, `/webhooks`, `/health`)
- `public/sitemap.xml`

## ⬜ Your setup tasks (only you can do)
1. **Custom domain** → point it at Render, then **update the domain** in: `index.html`
   (canonical + OG URLs), `robots.txt`, `sitemap.xml`. (`onrender.com` URLs barely rank.)
2. **Google Search Console** — verify the domain, submit `sitemap.xml`.
3. **Bing Webmaster Tools** — same.
4. **Google Business Profile** for Remi (company) — strong for local intent.
5. **Analytics** — GA4 or privacy-friendly Plausible (POPIA-friendlier).

**Problem-aware (blog/top-of-funnel):**

## Off-page / other channels
- **Backlinks:** SA business directories, aesthetics-industry blogs, partnerships, local press.
- **LinkedIn:** founder posts on missed-call/no-show pain (for B2B this often beats SEO in year 1).
- **Paid (faster than SEO):** Google Ads on "virtual receptionist" intent; Meta/IG ads to clinic owners; local Facebook groups for SA aesthetic practitioners.

## Measure
Search Console (impressions/clicks/positions), GA4/Plausible (traffic → demo bookings),
a simple rank tracker for the commercial keywords. The metric that matters: **demo
requests from organic**, not raw traffic.
