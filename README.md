# Remi

**An AI front desk for clinics** — answers every call and WhatsApp, books clients,
cuts no-shows, refills cancellations, and reports the revenue it recovered.

- **Status:** Pre-build (planning).
- **First market:** owner-run aesthetic & skin clinics — George & Cape Town, South Africa.
- **Model:** done-for-you; free 2-week parallel trial → ~R3,000–8,000/month.
- **Stack:** Twilio (WhatsApp) · Claude · Supabase · controlled calendar.
